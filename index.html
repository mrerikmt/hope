Our Mission

We know that climate change is an unfortunate reality for our future, but maybe we can figure out how to live with the changes we can't stop, and fix the things we can. Humans, plants and animals are going to have to evolve to meet the challenges ahead. There are already ways we can do this, like re-seeding coral reefs. Maybe there's more! We want to help the organizations that are leading the way!

The Earth Doesn't Need Us

It has all the time in the universe to work things out, but humans need the earth's climate to remain close to how it is now.

We Need To Give Ourselves Time

...to change our habits, and develop new technologies before we pass the point of no return.

Action is being taken

Efforts are underway all over the world to save species and lower our carbon footprint.

We can do it!

Humans are amazing but we need everyone to get involved, so join us in the effort to keep the earth beautiful!

How To Help

You probably don't have time to hunt down illegal whaling operations and (let's face it) you're not going to develop a breakthrough carbon sequestering technology. You can recycle, and try to drive less but it's not enough. Change has to happen with governments, corporations and the way we consume. There's no shortage of great people trying to make these changes, but they need help and one of the best ways to help them is to donate money to their organizations. Yep...money; the greatest unnatural resource on the planet.

Money makes things happen

From lobbying governments to research and development funding is how things get done and every little bit helps.

Give what you can

Not everyone needs to fund an expedition to the antarctic to study melting sea ice, but everyone can donate a little bit, and if everyone does then we can make a difference.

Priorities

$1 billion dollars was donated to rebuild Notre Dame Cathedral in just a few short weeks but nearly every organization devoted to slowing climate change struggles with funding. Where do your priorities lie?

Current Project

Oregon Zoo

The zoo is working hard to preserve our natural world. Covid forced them to limit funding for the important programs they support. Lets help them get back on their feet so they can keep making a difference!

1

Where We Stand
The Oregon Zoo works around the world on issues that affect biodiversity and the health of the planet.

2

Future For Wildlife
An ongoing partnership between the Oregon Zoo and the Oregon Zoo Foundation, the Future for Wildlife program provides grants to support local and global conservation efforts.

3

Fighting Extinction
The zoo and its partners work to recover imperiled native species ranging from tiny butterflies to California condors.

We want our planet to not just remain livable, but provide all the opportunities that it has to past generations. All of us love the outdoors and we want to keep enjoying our time in nature. Help us make sure we have a climate that we can count on to provide the same safety and stability that our ancestors had!
